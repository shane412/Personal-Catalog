Sample Schedule

This is a tentative pathway for a student aspiring to major in Public & Professional Writing.

First Year

Fall Semester

  • MATH 0220: ANALYTIC GEOMETRY AND CALCULUS 1 - 4 credits
  • CHEM 0960: GENERAL CHEM FOR ENGINEERS 1 - 3 credits
  • PHYS 0174: BASIC PHYSICS, SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING 1 (INTEGRATED) - 4 credits
  • ENGR 0011: INTRO TO ENGINEERING ANALYSIS - 3 credits
  • ENGR 0081: FRESHMAN ENGINEERING SEMINAR 1 - 0 credits
  • ENGCMP 0210: SEMINAR IN COMPOSITION: ENGINEERING - 3 credits

Spring Semester

  • MATH 0230: ANALYTIC GEOMETRY AND CALCULUS 2 - 4 credits
  • CHEM 0970: GENERAL CHEM FOR ENGINEERS 2 - 3 credits
  • PHYS 0175: BASIC PHYSICS, SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING 2 (INTEGRATED) - 4 credits
  • ENGR 0012: INTRO TO ENGINEERING COMPUTING - 3 credits
  • ENGR 0082: FRESHMAN ENGINEERING SEMINAR 2 - 0 credits
  • ENGCMP 0412: ENGINEERING COMMUNICATION IN A PROFESSIONAL CONTEXT - 3 credits

Second Year

Fall Semester

  • ENGCMP 1101: Language of Science and Technology - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 1104: Creative Corporate Writing - 3 credits
  • HIST 0400: East Asian Civilization to 1800 - 3 credits
  • HIST 0500: Colonial Latin America - 3 credits

Spring Semester

  • ENGCMP 0440 Critical Writing - 3 credits
  • CHEM 0110: General Chemistry 1 - 4 credits
  • GEOL 0840: Environmental Science - 3 credits
  • INTEREST/MINOR REQUIREMENT - 3 credits

Third Year

Fall Semester

  • GER 0101: Beginning German 1 - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 1900: PPW Internship - 3 credits
  • BIOSC 0150: Foundations of Biology 1 - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 0400: Written Professional Communication - 3 credits

Spring Semester

  • GER 0102: Beginning German 2 - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 0425: Digital Humanity - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 0420: Writing for the Public - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 0560: Writing Arguments - 3 credits

Fourth Year

Fall Semester

  • ENGCMP 0600: Intro to Technical Writing - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 1551: Hist. and Politics of the Eng. Language - 3 credits
  • HIST 1051: Catholicism in the New World - 3 credits
  • ENGCMP 1103: Public Relations Writing - 3 credits

Spring Semester

  • ENGCMP 1910 Bridge Seminar - 3 credits
  • HIST 0700: World History - 3 credits
  • INTEREST/MINOR REQUIREMENT - 3 credits
  • INTEREST/MINOR REQUIREMENT - 3 credits
